# App A name candidates

A workshop list for the RC1 rebrand. Per your direction: preserve both
themes — _Playing in the Sandbox_ × _Staring at Flowers_ — mixed
accordingly. Web/domain availability is out of scope (no website for
RC1).

Vibe spread, from playful → academic → ritual:

| # | Name | Vibe | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Specimen Sandbox** | studious + playful | Strong fit with the foil-stamped specimen-card metaphor. Preserves "sandbox" as the experimentation space; "specimen" anchors the botanical. |
| 2 | **Sandbox & Bloom** | balanced | Ampersand-joined, both themes equal weight. Short. Reads cleanly on small icon labels. |
| 3 | **Field Studies** | academic + observational | Drops both phrase words; preserves the _meaning_ (regional, careful, slow). Evokes a research notebook. Risk: generic. |
| 4 | **Daily Specimen** | ritual-first | Names the cadence + the artifact. Drops "sandbox". |
| 5 | **Petals in the Sandbox** | playful + long | Closest to original phrase rhythm. Reads narratively; longer for icon label. |
| 6 | **Flowerplay** | compressed + whimsical | Single coined word. Leans whimsical. Easy to trademark. |
| 7 | **Bloom Sandbox** | botanical-first | Swap order of "Specimen Sandbox". Lighter. |
| 8 | **Wildflower Workshop** | maker-vibe | "Workshop" stands in for "sandbox" (a place to work). "Wildflower" makes the native-species angle explicit. |
| 9 | **Today's Bloom** | ritual-first | Most descriptive of the function. Loses the playful angle entirely. |
| 10 | **Specimen of the Day** | collection-card framing | Card-game / trading-card energy. Implies collecting over time. |
| 11 | **Sandbox of Wildflowers** | long-form | Direct grammatical descendant of the current name. |
| 12 | **Staring at Flowers** | contemplative-only | Drop the "Sandbox" half, keep the slow-down half. Risk: loses the playful/experimental thread. |

## How to pick

Sit with the top 3-4 for a few minutes — read each aloud, imagine it on
an icon, in a sentence ("I built this little thing called ___"), as the
title of an App Store listing.

Pragmatic constraints to weight:

- **App Store name** must be ≤30 chars (most of these fit, #11 = 22).
- **Apple display name** can differ from the bundle ID — slug stays
  `playing-in-the-sandbox-staring-at-flowers` for RC1 (changing it would
  reset the EAS Update URL).
- **Icon label** ≤12 chars renders without truncation on iOS.
- **Searchability** — generic names (#3, #9) are harder to find.

## My pick (if forced)

**1. Specimen Sandbox** — it matches the foil-stamped card metaphor that
ran through the iridescent design phase, preserves both phrase themes,
reads cleanly at icon and listing scale, and has a strong differentiated
identity. Second choice: **2. Sandbox & Bloom** for its balance and
brevity.

But this is your call. Pick whichever feels right when you say it back
to yourself.
