Privacy Policy for Sandbox Flowers

Last Updated: April 25, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Sandbox Flowers ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

Location Data

The app requests your device's approximate location once — the first time you open it — in order to determine your US state. This is used solely to select flowers native to your region. The resolved state code (e.g., "CA") is stored on your device only and is never transmitted to us or any third party. You can reset this at any time by clearing the app's local storage.

Data Collection and Usage

Beyond the one-time region lookup described above, the app operates with a strict "no data collection" philosophy:

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not track your usage
  • We do not store any information on remote servers
  • We do not use analytics services
  • We do not serve advertisements

Local Device Storage

Your resolved region (US state code) is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Daily flower images and descriptions are fetched from GitHub Pages (a static CDN). No identifying information is included in these requests beyond a standard HTTP request.

Children's Privacy

Our application is suitable for all ages and does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will provide notice as appropriate under the circumstances, such as displaying a prominent notice within the App or on this page.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the GitHub Issues page for this project.

Effective Date

This policy is effective as of April 10, 2025.
