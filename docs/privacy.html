Privacy Policy for Sandbox Flowers

Last Updated: April 10, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Sandbox Flowers ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

Data Collection and Usage

Our app does not collect any personal information. We operate with a strict "no data collection" philosophy:

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not track your usage
  • We do not store any information on remote servers
  • We do not use analytics services
  • We do not serve advertisements

Local Device Storage

Any data created within the app is stored locally on your device only. This includes any preferences or creations made within the sandbox environment. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with or utilize any third-party services that would collect or process your data.

Children's Privacy

Our application is suitable for all ages and does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will provide notice as appropriate under the circumstances, such as displaying a prominent notice within the App or on this page.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the GitHub Issues page for this project.

Effective Date

This policy is effective as of April 10, 2025.
